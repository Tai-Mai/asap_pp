ASAP++: Attribute Specific Essay Grading

Automated essay grading is one of the most important educational of NLP. The goal of the system is to score the essay, either overall, or for particular aspects of essays, like content, organization, style, etc. One of the most commonly used datasets in essay grading is the Automatic Student Assessment Prize (ASAP) Automatic Essay Grading (AEG) dataset.

This page is where we release our resource and other accompanying software.
